Wake App

"More than just an alarm clock"

Wake App is an innovative alarm clock application designed to engage your mind actively as you start your day. Unlike traditional alarm clocks, Wake App incorporates a unique feature set that challenges users to fully awaken their cognitive skills before they can silence the alarm.

Features


Trivia Time!

For those who prefer a different kind of mental stimulation, Wake App includes a trivia mode. This feature covers a wide range of topics, from general knowledge to specific areas of interest. Answering a set of trivia questions correctly is required to turn off the alarm, ensuring that the user is fully alert.


Math N' Dash!

When the alarm rings, Wake App presents a series of math questions of varying difficulty. These problems are designed to stimulate the user's problem-solving and analytical skills. From simple arithmetic to more complex equations, the level of difficulty can be customized according to the user's preference.


Scan N' Go

This innovative feature requires users to get out of bed and scan a predetermined item, such as a barcode in the kitchen or a book in the living room. This physical activity, combined with the mental task of locating and scanning the item, guarantees that the user is fully awake.
